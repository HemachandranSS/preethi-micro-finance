üìÖ Timeline: Evolution of Indian Savings Behavior

1990s-2000s

The Golden Era of Bank Deposits

High FD rates (8-11%), strong preference for safe instruments, gold investments dominated, risk-averse mentality.

2010-2015

Transition Phase

Introduction of SIP culture, growing awareness of equity markets, real estate boom, gradual shift from savings to investments.

2016-2020

Digital Revolution

Demonetization impact, digital payments explosion, easier access to mutual funds, fintech disruption.

2020-Present

New Age Investors

Pandemic-driven equity boom, crypto curiosity, younger generation debt-tolerant, returns prioritized over safety.
